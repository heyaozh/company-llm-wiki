# Concepts

Atomic, reusable, **model-agnostic** primitives distilled from methodology documents — the
shared-vocabulary layer beneath the wiki. A concept is the company analogue of a research
"concept" note: one idea, one file, linked from every methodology / specification that uses it.

Each document is `concept-<slug>.md` and follows [`../SCHEMA.md`](../SCHEMA.md) §5.5. Copy
[`_TEMPLATE.md`](_TEMPLATE.md) to begin. See [`../CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md) §5 for the
distillation rule (one concept per file; merge, don't duplicate).
